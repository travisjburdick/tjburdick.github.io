History

EVE Aura was developed by Jason Parks over at Aideron Robotics. It's the only Application he currently has on the market place.

The Application itself simply serves as an away from Game Utility program, a way to keep track of everything you may want to for the complex game EVE Online.

EVE Online is developed by Crowd Control Productions (CCP) Games. CCP is an Icelandic video game studio who's most well known game is EVE Online itself, though they have recently launched DUST 514, which is also set in the EVE universe.

EVE Online is a unique in the current Market Place of online games, having won multiple MMORPG Game of the Year Awards throughout it's relatively long running history. While it's not as massive as some of it's competitors, it's got a rather loyal fanbase.

  • SuperPlay GULDPIXELN 2003: Online Game of the year
