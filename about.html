A little about me...

First, the obvious. I'm into design and development, both! I love problem solving, and getting the chance to be creative.

As for the rest, I'm originally from New York, and now reside in Florida. Here, I'm currently attending school and working. The whole goal is to broaden my own horizons, and expand on my skill set.

I'm always looking to improve, so most of my hobbies run along those lines. Reading, working on my own projects, writing, and studying new subjects! There's always more to know, and ways to get better. Though, I'm sure it seems obvious with the theme of this portfolio, I have a rather large interest in space, it's complexity and simplicity both. It's something I try to bring to my design, simple, beautiful, efficient.
